CanaKit/RetroPie Guide

Download NES and SNES ROMs from:

https://nblog.org/rompacks-romsets/

&

Sega ROMs from:

https://www.reddit.com/r/Roms/comments/abz20p/sega_genesis_usa_complete_romset_every_usa_game/

Additional ROMs can be added later!

Extract downloaded files.

To flatten folders, cd space to upper directory and run:

for /r %f in (*) do @copy "%f" .

ROM Suffix explanations:

https://64bitorless.wordpress.com/rom-suffix-explanations/

Apply heat sinks to board, see how board fits in case first to get a good idea of where the heat sinks will come through - they can be tight.

Use guide here for install of OS:

https://retropie.org.uk/docs/First-Installation/

Uncomment the following two lines in config.txt:

hdmi_force_hotplug=1

hdmi_drive=2
